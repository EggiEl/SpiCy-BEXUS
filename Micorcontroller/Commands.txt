General Structure:

Commands can be used to alter variables or read out data.
Read out data will be returned with the next struct in the info array.
Commands and data are seperated via "\n"

char | char | char | ":" | char[16] | "\n" |
Command_A | Command_B | Command_C | Seperator | Param | end

Param is Command dependent but max. 16 byte long

Read out Startuptests:
Command: sro:"\n"
Return: sro | 32 bit |
